Our world today is plagued by ecological destruction, political greed, and social division. The time has come to rise—not with destruction—but with visionary transformation. This mandate is not just an ideology; it's a new way of life, a constitution for Earth based on science, reason, ecology, and human harmony.

Ten Foundational Principles of EHM

  1. Science and ecological balance are the supreme laws of the land.
  2. All humans are equal; caste, race, and religion have no say in policy or law.
  3. Power is given only to those who understand and follow ecological truths.
  4. Religion is private and sacred; it must never cross into politics or lawmaking.
  5. Greed in any form is punishable. A society of takers collapses; we are givers.
  6. Natural affection, love, and emotional well-being are encouraged in safe, respectful ways.
  7. The Earth is sacred. Any exploitation of nature without regeneration is banned.
  8. Weapons and war must be replaced with education and innovation.
  9. Leadership is earned through vision, not wealth or inheritance.
  10. Voices are heard. But those that disturb nature or divide society will be corrected—with peace or power.
