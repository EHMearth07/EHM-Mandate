About Zeonergia

Zeonergia is a bold initiative under Zeotech that envisions a world powered by sustainable bioenergy derived from microbial innovations. We believe that by tapping into the vast potential of engineered microorganisms, we can reduce dependency on fossil fuels and usher in a cleaner, greener energy age.

The Science

At the heart of Zeonergia lies synthetic biology, microbiology, and ecological biochemistry. Our goal is to design bacteria, algae, and yeast strains that can convert biomass, waste, or atmospheric CO₂ into usable biofuels such as ethanol, butanol, hydrogen, or even advanced hydrocarbons, mimicking fossil fuels.

Vision & Goals

  • Create modular biofuel generation systems deployable in rural areas
  • Integrate biofuel tech into smart cities and future transport systems
  • Collaborate with Indian agri and waste management sectors
  • Build a student-researcher driven ecosystem for open innovation

Ecological Impact

Zeonergia aims to drastically lower the carbon footprint by converting biological waste into energy, minimizing landfill load, and reducing fossil fuel usage. The by-products of many microbial biofuel processes are non-toxic and can serve as fertilizers or soil conditioners.
